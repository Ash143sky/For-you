Poetry for My Cherished Friend

"In the garden of my heart, blooms a friendship true and rare,
With you, my dear friend, a bond beyond compare.

Through laughter and tears, through joy and strife,
Your presence brings sunshine into my life.

With each passing day, our friendship grows,
Like a river that forever flows.

Cherished moments we've shared, memories so dear,
In my heart, you'll always be near.

So here's to you, my friend, with love that's pure and true,
For all that you are and all that you do.

Forever grateful, forever blessed,
With you, my dear friend, I've found happiness.
